Origins

Snowboarding began in the 1960s when Sherman Poppen created the “Snurfer.”

Major Milestones

  1. 2022 – Snowboarding continues Olympic dominance
  2. 2014 – Slopestyle added to Olympics
  3. 1998 – Olympic debut in Nagano
  4. 1980s – Resorts begin allowing snowboarders
  5. 1965 – The Snurfer is invented

Growth in Popularity

During the 1980s and 1990s, snowboarding grew rapidly and became an Olympic sport in 1998.

Snowboarding made its Olympic debut at the 1998 Winter Games in Nagano.

Olympic Debut

Snowboarding officially became an Olympic sport on February 8, 1998.
